Denise Potter RD, CSP, CDE

Denise has been a Registered Dietitian for 24 years. She has been administering the Ketogenic Diet to children with epilepsy and other conditions at the University of Michigan CS Mott Children's Hospital for 7 years. Prior to her interest in the Ketogenic Diet, she specialized in Diabetes Education. She has been a CDE (Certified Diabetes Educator) for 17 years.

Most recently she attained the CSP credential (Certified Specialist in Pediatrics).

Denise received her BS in Dietetics from Michigan State University and completed her Dietetic Internship at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences.

Ketogenic diet program for cancer patients

An experimental dietary approach to treating cancer

Ketogenic Therapies, LLC provides a 6-month, medically monitored ketogenic diet program for cancer patients. The program is run by a Registered Dietitian (RD) with 24 years of experience in nutrition and 7 years with the Ketogenic Diet.

A high fat, low carbohydrate diet

The Ketogenic Diet (KGD) is a high fat, moderate protein, low carbohydrate diet which is thought to starve cancer cells by depriving them of glucose. The diet is very concise and is most effective with very high compliance.

Keto kit included

A “Keto Kit” will be provided to assist patients with all of the start-up equipment needed.

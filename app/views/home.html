Ketogenic diet program for cancer patients

Ketogenic Therapies, LLC has been formed with the intention of offering cancer patients a one year, medically monitored program to follow for experimental cancer treatment. The program is run by a Registered Dietitian (RD) with 24 years of experience in nutrition and 7 years of experience with the Ketogenic Diet.

The Ketogenic Diet (KGD) is a high fat, moderate protein, low carbohydrate diet. The diet is very concise and is most effective with very high compliance.

A “Keto Kit” will be provided to assist patients with all of the start-up equipment needed.

If you or someone you love is interested in learning more about the KGD for cancer treatment, please fill out the contact form and you will receive a free 15-minute phone call from the dietitian to help you understand the program.

Ketogenic Diet Kit

Everything you need to get started

Each program participant will receive a keto kit to get them off to a good start on the program. The kit includes the following:

  • Precision Xtra Meter
  • Precision Xtra blood ketone test strips (30)
  • Precision Xtra blood glucose test strips (60)
  • Gram scale
  • Pinch bowls
  • Mini spatulas
  • KetoCal 4:1 Liquid
  • KetoCal 4:1 Powder
  • KetoCal 3:1 Powder
  • Ketogenic Therapies Tote bag

Cost of the kit is offset by product donations from Abbott Diabetes Care and Nutricia North America.

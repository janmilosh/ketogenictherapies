Programs/services available

Contact us for a free 30 minute phone consultation for general information. All phone and email consultations are with a Registered Dietitian.

3 Month Program includes:

  • Initial assessment
  • Ketogenic Diet Instruction:
    • Meal planning and schedule
    • Ketodietcalculator training
    • Scale and meter training
  • Daily phone/email contact during diet initiation (7-10 days)
  • Weekly phone call and daily email as needed for weeks 2-4
  • Monthly follow-up calls and 1-2 emails/week for months 2-3

1 Month Program (for those already on a Ketogenic Diet):

  • Evaluation of current Ketogenic Diet
  • Glucose/ketone monitoring
  • Weekly phone contact
  • Email support

Monthly monitoring for program participants:

  • After completion of 3-month program
  • 30-60 minute phone follow-up
  • 2 emails per week
  • Lab review as needed

Phone consultation:

  • 60 minute call

Fees:

  • 3 Month Ketogenic Diet Initiation: $1550
  • 1 Month Ketogenic Diet Program: $700
  • Monthly Monitoring: $100/month
  • Non-program phone consults, 60 minutes: $175

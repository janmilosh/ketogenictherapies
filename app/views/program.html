Program

Contact us for a free 15 minute phone consultation for general information. If you wish to discuss your case further, there is an initial phone consultation for up to 45 minutes for a fee of $100. This is applied to the program fee if enrolled. (Note: all phone/email consultations and classes are with a Registered Dietician.)

The program includes:

  • Physician contact (by RD)
  • Dietary intake evaluation
  • Ratio determination
  • Needs assessment: Calorie, nutrient, and fluid requirements
  • History and Physical Review
  • Lab review at 3 days, 7 days, 14 days, monthly and quarterly as described
  • Group or individual instruction classes, which include:
    • Ketocalculator training
    • Scale training
    • Glucose/ketone meter training
    • Diet overview
    • Meal planning and schedule
  • Menu assistance
  • Daily phone/email contact during diet initiation (7-10 days)
  • 15-30 minute phone contact and 2 emails/week for weeks 2-6
  • 30-60 minute monthly follow-up calls and 1 email/week for months 2-6
  • 30-60 minute phone follow-up at months 9 and 12 and 3 emails/month for months 7-12
  • Lab review at months 9 and 12
  • Monthly progress reports to your physician
  • Keto Kit

Fees:

  • Individual: $3150
  • Group Class (3-6 patients): Discounted by $100
  • Group Class (7-12 patients): Discounted by $200
  • Non-program phone consults, 45 minutes: $100

How do I sign up?

  1. Fill out the contact form and schedule your free 15 minute informational call.
  2. Discuss the Ketogenic Diet with your physician.
  3. Send the referral form and diet description to your physician.
  4. Schedule your initial diet overview consultation call.
  5. Accurately track your food and fluid intake for 3 days on www.livestrong.com/myplate or a similar website and email to your dietician.
  6. Once physician referral has been returned, schedule class or individual session. In person education is desired, but Skype is available for long-distance participants.
  7. Get your fasting lab work done and results emailed to the dietician as soon as possible.
  8. Review www.charliefoundation.org for additional information about the KGD.
